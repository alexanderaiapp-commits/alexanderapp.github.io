Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

Alexander AI respects your privacy. We do not sell data, do not store images on our servers, and do not share personal information with third parties for advertising purposes.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Alexander AI, we may collect:

  • Email address and user identifier (via Google Sign-In)
  • Access to your Google Calendar — solely for the purpose of adding events
  • Screenshots you choose to scan — processed and immediately deleted
  • Basic usage data for service improvement
  • Expo Push Token for sending notifications

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively to:

  • Identify shifts and events from screenshots
  • Add events to your Google Calendar
  • Send push notifications about detected events
  • Improve model accuracy in the future

3. Images and Screenshots

Images submitted for scanning are processed in real time by Gemini AI and are never stored on Alexander's servers. After extracting the information, the image is immediately deleted.

4. Sharing with Third Parties

We do not sell, lease, or share personal information with third parties, except:

  • Google Calendar API — to add events to your calendar
  • Google Gemini AI — for image processing (without storage)
  • Expo / Firebase — for push notifications
  • When required by law

5. Data Security

All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS). The server runs on Google Cloud Run with restricted access controls. We apply the principle of minimum permissions for data access.

6. Data Retention

User data (calendar settings, preferences, categories) is stored in Firestore and associated with your Google account. You may request deletion of all your data at any time.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to request:

  • Access to the personal data collected about you
  • Deletion of all your data from our system
  • Correction of inaccurate information

Contact us at: alexanderaiapp@gmail.com

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be announced in the app. Continued use constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

For any privacy-related questions: alexanderaiapp@gmail.com
